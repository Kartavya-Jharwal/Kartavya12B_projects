Modular Arithmetic
& Cryptography

Applied Mathematics (241)
Academic year 2023-24

In this project, I will explore modular arithmetic, covering its core definitions,
historical context, mathematical principles, and practical examples,
followed by a brief overview of its application in cryptography,
concluding with a practical implementation using Excel.
